## üá¶üá™ What is the SCA (Securities and Commodities Authority)?

The **SCA** is the official financial regulator in the UAE, overseeing capital markets and ensuring that all registered entities comply with local laws.

SCA-regulated forex brokers must:

* Offer transparent trading conditions
* Report activities to the SCA
